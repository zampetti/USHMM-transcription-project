RG-50.030*0217 PREFACE The following oral history testimony is the result of a taped interview with Stanislaw Soszynski, conducted on October 6, 1989 on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum. The interview is part of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's collection of oral testimonies. Rights to the interview are held by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum.
The reader should bear in mind that this is a verbatim transcript of spoken, rather than written prose. This transcript has been neither checked for spelling nor verified for accuracy, and therefore, it is possible that there are errors. As a result, nothing should be quoted or used from this transcript without first checking it against the taped interview.
STANISLAW SOSZYNSKI
October 6, 1989
