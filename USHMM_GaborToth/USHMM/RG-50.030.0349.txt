United States Holocaust Memorial MuseumPRIVATE Interview with Dr. Jacques Godel
August 28, 1995
RG-50.030*0349 PREFACE The following oral history testimony is the result of a taped interview with Dr. Jacques Godel, conducted on August 28, 1995 on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum. The interview is part of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's collection of oral testimonies. Rights to the interview are held by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum.
The reader should bear in mind that this is a verbatim transcript of spoken, rather than written prose. This transcript has been neither checked for spelling nor verified for accuracy, and therefore, it is possible that there are errors. As a result, nothing should be quoted or used from this transcript without first checking it against the taped interview.
DR. JACQUES GODEL
August 28, 1995 QUESTION: Where you were born. Date of birth.
ANSWER: My name is Jaques Godel. I was born October the 9th, 1924, in Paris, France.
Q: Tell me a little bit about your familyyour family and your life before the war.
