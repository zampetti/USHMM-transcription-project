RG-50.106*0109 PREFACE The following oral history testimony is the result of an audiotaped interview with Lena Jurand, conducted by Gail Schwartz on August 15, 1998 on behalf of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum. The interview took place in Washington, D.C. and is part of the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum's collection of oral testimonies. Rights to the interview are held by the United States Holocaust Memorial Museum.
The reader should bear in mind that this is a verbatim transcript of spoken, rather than written prose. This transcript has been neither checked for spelling nor verified for accuracy, and therefore, it is possible that there are errors. As a result, nothing should be quoted or used from this transcript without first checking it against the taped interview.
LENA JURANDPRIVATE
August 15, 1998
