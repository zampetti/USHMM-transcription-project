CONTENTSI was born in Sarnzevo in Yugoslovia, a large city with many Jews.

Hitler sent student spies to proogandize the people and cause trouble. We were occupied by the Italians in the late 30s. In 1941 the Germans came in.

There were seven children in my family, I came from a very religious family, kept kosher, holidays, rituals. My mother came from a very rich religious family. My mother begged money from her father for our education. After high school I went to school for two years for business in the German language. The Rabbi came into the Cloister where my school met to give lessons in Hebrew. We were on very good terms with the Catholics. Our maids were Catholic. There was no antiâ€‘Semitism.

(She spoke at length of how good they were to their servants).

When it looked like there would be a war, we found the family was separated and living far from each other. My sister was married to a Catholic and her husband did not want to hide my brother . The Germans picked up Jews and put them in cattle cars. (She has an attack of sobbing). My family was mostly wiped out.

When Hitler came in, I was living with my husband and my 12 year old son in Spanito. We had an olive oil factory. My son is an engineer, working on the Ivory Coast. The Germans did not allow my son to continue school. We sent him to a private tutor. The priest told us the Germans were coming. We left for Italy in Nov. 1943 and stayed there until 1947. First we were in Barrie and then left for Torino. My son had sent an application to MIT and had received a scholarship. We emigrated to the U.S When we went to Italy, it took us 15 days to get there by night. We took very little with us.

Friends got us an apartment. (Again uncontrolled sobbing).

We went to a refugee camp in Barrie.
